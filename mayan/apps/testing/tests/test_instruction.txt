Dashboard{

Fature: The dashboard is used to serve as a resume management tool that makes both 
reviewers and candidate easier to view and manage the applications.

Manual Testing: After logging into the Mayan-EDMS system, the user should see a dashboard tag on
left sidebar menu. Clicking on the tag will lead the user to the dashboard interface. Reviewers can
assign ratings on recommendation letters, interviews, essays and extracurricular activities of a
candidate by typing in the corresponding field. Candidates can update their GPA, undergraduate school 
and undergraduate major similarly. For each document listed, the reviewers can change the status by 
clicking on the drop-down menu and select from one of the status: Unassigned, processing and unprocessed.
Documents that belong to different categories should appear in different columns.
